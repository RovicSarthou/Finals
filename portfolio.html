About Me

Hello! I am Rovic Sarthou, a Gemologist with a passion for gemstones and fine jewelry. With 7 years of experience in the jewelry industry, I specialize in gemstone identification, appraisal, and jewelry design.

I am dedicated to combining craftsmanship with a deep understanding of gemology to create exquisite pieces that resonate with elegance and quality.

Skills

Gemstone Identification

Ability to identify gemstones based on their physical and optical properties, including color, refractive index, and inclusions.

Jewelry Design

Knowledge of jewelry design principles and techniques to create aesthetically pleasing and functional jewelry pieces.

Market Knowledge

Awareness of market trends, prices, and consumer preferences in gemstones and jewelry.

Projects

Gem Identification

Utilizes a range of tools and techniques such as magnification, spectroscopy, and refractometry to identify gemstones accurately.

Jewelry Design

Translates ideas and inspirations into preliminary sketches and designs, considering aesthetic appeal and functionality.

Jewelry Appraisal

Utilizes expertise to evaluate jewelry based on factors like material composition (e.g., precious metals, gemstones), craftsmanship, brand reputation, and market demand.
